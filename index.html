Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Slap Phone: Prank Sounds ("the App") is developed by cryingonion. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All user preferences (sensitivity settings, sound pack selections, slap counts) are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore and are never sent to any server.

Sensor Data

The App uses your device's accelerometer to detect physical impacts ("slaps"). This sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings under Google > Ads.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - Advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing - In-app purchases (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Asset Delivery - Sound pack delivery

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to retain. Local preferences on your device persist until you clear the app's data or uninstall it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
